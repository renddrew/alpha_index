Abyssinian

The Abyssinian is known for its distinctive ticked coat. They are active and playful.

American Bobtail

A breed with a unique bobbed tail, the American Bobtail is playful and intelligent.

Bengal

The Bengal cat has a distinctive spotted coat and is known for its wild appearance.

Birman

Birmans are affectionate and have a silky coat with color-point patterns.

British Shorthair

Known for its round face and dense coat, the British Shorthair is calm and easygoing.

Calico

Calico isn't a breed but a color pattern. Calico cats have a mix of three colors: white, black, and orange.

Cornish Rex

The Cornish Rex is known for its curly coat and playful nature.

Devon Rex

Similar to the Cornish Rex, the Devon Rex has a curly coat but is smaller in size.

Exotic Shorthair

This breed is similar to the Persian but has a short coat. They are calm and affectionate.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They have tufted ears and a bushy tail.

Manx

The Manx is known for its taillessness or having a very short tail.

Norwegian Forest Cat

Originating from Northern Europe, this breed has a thick coat suitable for cold climates.

Oriental

The Oriental breed is closely related to the Siamese and has a sleek appearance.

Persian

Persians are known for their long, silky coats and flat faces. They are calm and enjoy lounging.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are large, affectionate cats that often go limp when you pick them up, hence the name.

Scottish Fold

Known for its unique folded ears, the Scottish Fold is playful and affectionate.

Sphynx

The Sphynx is a hairless breed known for its wrinkled skin and large ears.

Siamese

Siamese cats are known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes, short coat, and color-point patterns.

Turkish Angora

Originating from Turkey, this breed has a silky coat and is known for its playful nature.
